# GoldenHour AI

## AI-Powered Emergency Response Platform

GoldenHour AI is an intelligent emergency response platform designed to reduce the time between a road accident and medical treatment.

The platform helps citizens, hospitals, emergency responders, and government agencies coordinate faster during accident situations.

---

## Problem

Thousands of accident victims lose valuable treatment time because emergency services are not informed quickly enough.

Delays in:
- Accident reporting
- Severity assessment
- Hospital selection
- Emergency communication

can significantly reduce survival chances.

---

## Solution

GoldenHour AI provides:

- Accident Reporting System
- AI Severity Prediction
- Emergency Alert Network
- Hospital Recommendation Engine
- AI First Aid Assistant
- Government Analytics Dashboard

---

## Target Users

- Citizens
- Accident Witnesses
- Ambulance Services
- Hospitals
- Traffic Police
- Government Agencies
